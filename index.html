Sean

I first learned to code at Indiana University in 1980 using Fortran and IBM punch cards. It took twenty minutes to wait in line for cards to be run only to get a paper printout indicating an error on line 10 of 400. Back in line to repunch card 10. What a pain!

Forty years later I'm a partner in a small digital marketing agency, Clever Dogs Media, and spend some of my free time hiking and looking for bears in Yellowstone.

Hazel
